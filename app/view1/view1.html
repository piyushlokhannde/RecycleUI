Reward At Each Sprint



Sprinter helps evaluating the performance of the team during sprint. Your hard work will not go unnoticed. Sprinter captures every bit of it. It encourages teams to work in collabration and deliver above what is expected.

It captures the hardship of the team during sprint and convert them into numbers. You get additional point for your extra contribution. You can increaes the score of the team by contrbuting more.

Team points get divided into team members. Helps you to keep yourself ahead of others.
